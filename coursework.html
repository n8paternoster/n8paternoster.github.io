Undergraduate Coursework

A sample of some of my coursework from my B.S. in Music Engineering at the University of Miami's Frost School of Music (2011-2015). Coursework is broken down by discipline.

  • Digital Audio

    Digital signal processing concepts centered around audio; includes projects in MATLAB demonstrating the sampling theorem, quantization, the FFT, and convolution

  • Transducer Theory

    Fundamentals of electromagnetism, acoustics, and audio transducer theory including analysis and design of loudspeaker and microphone systems with a final project of building a speaker

  • Audio Electronics

    Study of the electronic circuits of filters and other audio circuits, all projects designed and modeled in Circuitmaker

  • Audio Postproduction

    Video technology and sound design with a focus on audio postproduction and animation

  • Recording Services

    Recital recording and sound reinforcement with experience in recording, mixing, and editing student and faculty concerts

  • Data Structures

    A study of data structures including dynamic arrays, linked lists, hash tables, trees, heaps, stacks, and queues

  • Algorithms

    Object-oriented programming concepts such as classes, inheritance, polymorphism as well as algorithms including tree traversal, hashing, sorting (quicksort and mergesort), and recursion removal

  • IOS Programming

    IOS app development in Objective-C

  • Microprocessors

    Computer architecture, microprocessors, ARM assmembly, memory and caching, computer performance, and related topics

  • Circuits, Signals, and Systems

    Digital signal processing concepts of LTI systems including transient analysis of circuits, convolution, Laplace and Fourier transforms

  • Electronics I and II

    A study of semiconductors, diodes, BJT's, FET's, op-amps, integrated circuits, timers, and similar concepts; includes DC and AC analysis, frequency response, and all simulation done in Pspice

  • Digital Design Lab

    Digital electronics lab for designing, simulating, and building digital circuits such as a seven-segment display, shift register, and multiplier; circuits built on breadboards and simulated in ModelSim and on Altera Quartus programmable logic device

  • Structured Digital Design Lab

    Design, modeling, and simulation of digital circuits using VHDL

  • PCB Design

    Printed circuit board design using ExpressPCB
